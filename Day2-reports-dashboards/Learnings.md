# 📚 Learnings from Day 2

During Day 2 of the Salesforce Administrator Track, I learned how Salesforce Reports and Dashboards help organizations analyze data and make informed business decisions.

## 🔹 Reports

- Learned what Salesforce Reports are and how they organize data.
- Understood how reports help users filter, summarize, and analyze records.
- Learned different uses of reports in business operations.
- Explored report types and report folders.

## 🔹 Dashboards

- Learned what Salesforce Dashboards are.
- Understood how dashboards visually display report data.
- Explored dashboard components such as charts, tables, and metrics.
- Learned the purpose of dynamic dashboards.

## 🔹 Reports vs Dashboards

- Understood the differences between reports and dashboards.
- Learned when to use detailed reports and when to use dashboard summaries.
- Explored how dashboards are built using reports.

## 🔹 Business Analytics

- Learned how organizations use Salesforce data for business analytics.
- Understood how reports and dashboards support performance monitoring.
- Explored real-world reporting and KPI tracking.

## 🔹 KPI Tracking

- Learned the importance of Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).
- Understood how dashboards help management monitor KPIs.
- Explored performance measurement using reports and dashboards.

## 🔹 Management Reporting

- Learned how management uses dashboards to monitor business performance.
- Understood how trends and patterns can be identified through analytics.
- Learned how data supports strategic decision-making.

## 🔹 Enterprise Thinking

- Understood how dashboards help identify issues such as:
  - Low attendance
  - Decreasing placements
  - Low internship participation

- Learned how management can use insights from dashboards to take corrective actions.

## 🎯 Key Takeaway

Salesforce Reports and Dashboards convert raw data into actionable insights, enabling management to monitor performance, track KPIs, and make data-driven business decisions effectively.
